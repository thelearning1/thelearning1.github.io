How does changing Latitude affect the temperature?

The relationship between latitude and temperature is both the most obvious and the most interesting. Generally, the closer you are to the equator, the hotter the temperature gets. However, the Earth's rotational axis is tilted which provides us with these little things called seasons. The data plotted is from January 5th; Winter for places north of the equator and summer for places south. This is very easy to notice on the plot as the southern locations have temperatures on par or higher than those closer to the equator.

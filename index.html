Recorded and released in 2022, this album is the most progressive and darkest in J-Roses catalogue. Inspired and created through psychedelics, this album delves into human consciousness, nature and the inward journey. While the previous two albums had mostly bright and upbeat songs, this album is focused around the mysteries of the universe, cosmic insights, and the connection between mind, body and soul.
